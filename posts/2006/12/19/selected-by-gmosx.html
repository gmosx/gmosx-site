Selected by Gmosx

Amazon recently added a nice feature to its Associates Program: Astore. In essence, this allows you to build a customized store and sell items from Amazon’s vast product catalog. As I plan to review or reference a lot of books and other products in this Blog, I thought it was a good idea to prepare a nice little collection of carefully selected books, DVD’s, Music, Games, etc.

Well, welcome to ‘Selected by Gmosx’ then! If you are interested in programming, business, design, computers, games, music, movies, science and more you will find something you like here. Only the absolutely best products will be featured in this shop. Check back often to view the latest additions. And remember, when ordering from my store you are supporting my continuous work on numerous free projects.
